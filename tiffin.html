Brampton Tiffin

Brampton Tiffin is ultimate app designed exclusively for residents of Brampton, a vibrant city in Canada. With Brampton Tiffin, you can explore a diverse selection of tiffins prepared by talented local sellers, right at your fingertips.

App Features
  • Seller Management:
    • Seller Registration and Onboarding: Allow local sellers to register as vendors on the platform by providing their relevant information, such as contact details, business name, and location. Implement a verification process to ensure the authenticity of sellers.
    • Kitchen Creation: Enable sellers to create and manage their virtual kitchen within the app. Sellers can set up their kitchen profile, including kitchen name, description, cuisine specialties, etc.
    • Tiffin Management: Provide sellers with a user-friendly interface to add, edit, and delete their tiffin offerings. Sellers can include details such as tiffin name, description, ingredients, portion size, dietary information, pricing, and high-quality images.
  • Customer Interface:
    • Tiffin Exploration: Provide customers with a comprehensive view of all tiffins offered by local sellers in Brampton. Display a visually appealing layout showcasing tiffin images, names, prices, and seller information.
    • Seller Details: Allow customers to view details of each seller, including their kitchen information and their published tiffins. This helps customers make informed decisions when selecting a seller.
    • Direct Seller Contact: Provide customers with the ability to contact sellers directly through the app. Customers can initiate conversations with sellers to ask questions, request customizations, or seek additional information about the tiffins.
    • In-App Chat: Implement a chat feature that allows customers to communicate with sellers in real-time. Customers can discuss specific requirements, coordinate delivery details, or inquire about any ongoing promotions or discounts.
    • Support and Assistance: Offer a dedicated support system for sellers and customers, allowing them to reach out for any technical issues, inquiries, or guidance.
